Hello, World!

Maximilian Vidavsky

Hello, world! Welcome to my personal blog. I'm a sophomore at Penn State, studying Computer Science at the University Park campus. I'm also the president of the Slavic Club. I speak English and Russian fluently, and I'm pretty comfortable with Python. I'm also currently learning Java.

This blog will mostly be about the things I'm interested in: fintech, obsolete tech like pagers, and everything else in between! I enjoy writing about obscure topics and sharing them with a wider audience. I try to write in a way that is accessible to the average person.

If you have any feedback, feel free to shoot me a Twitter DM or an e-mail. I'll try to get back to you as soon as I can.

Enjoy the blog!
